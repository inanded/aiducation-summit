Keynote
Innovation with Integrity: Keeping Humans at the Heart of AI in Schools
Professor Rose Luckin
Professor Rose Luckin draws on over three decades of research to set out a human-centred framework for AI adoption. She explores why strategy without integrity leads to ungoverned risk, why governance without understanding leads to paralysis, and why innovation without purpose risks replacing the very human capabilities we should be developing.

Innovation Partners

Innovation Partner
Open Education AI

Formally launched in 2023 as a sector-led non-profit, Open Education AI solves the challenge of data fragmentation in schools. The platform automatically standardises information from over 30 EdTech companies and all major MIS providers into real-time dashboards. Currently used by more than 30 trusts and 600 schools, OEAI empowers educators to use predictive analytics to anticipate attendance risks and safeguarding vulnerabilities before they escalate.

Innovation Partner
Edequity AI

Edequity AI combines the trust and scale earned through a 20-year history of service in education IT with leading-edge development in data, analytics, and AI from the founders of Open Education AI. Multi-Academy Trusts, government bodies, and higher education institutions all recognise the potential for AI to positively impact education. Edequity AI has both the experience and vision to support your organisation at whatever stage of the digital journey.

Keynote
Innovation with Integrity: Keeping Humans at the Heart of AI in Schools
Professor Rose Luckin
Professor Rose Luckin draws on over three decades of research and her work with schools worldwide to set out a human-centred framework for AI adoption. She explores why strategy without integrity leads to “shadow AI” and ungoverned risk, why governance without understanding leads to paralysis, and why innovation without purpose risks replacing the very human capabilities we should be developing in our learners.
